2:1 ENCODER

The simplest encoder: two input lines compress into a single output bit. It also shows, most clearly, why plain encoders break when more than one input is active at once.

2:1 Encoder Simulator

Toggle Y1 and Y0 independently. Output A always mirrors Y1. Valid (V) tells you whether the current input combination is meaningful — try setting both to 1 to see the ambiguity a plain encoder cannot resolve.
